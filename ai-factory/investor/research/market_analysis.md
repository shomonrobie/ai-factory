## Market Trends

### Key Drivers
1. **AI adoption in software development**
   - 80% of developers use AI tools by 2028
   - 40% CAGR in AI coding tools

2. **Developer shortage**
   - 1M+ unfilled developer positions in US
   - 4M+ globally

3. **Time-to-market pressure**
   - 60% of startups fail due to slow development
   - MVP expectations: weeks, not months

4. **Cost optimization**
   - Average developer cost: $150k/year
   - AI tools can reduce costs by 50-70%

### Market Risks
1. **Competition** - Existing AI coding tools
2. **Regulation** - AI governance and compliance
3. **Adoption barriers** - Trust in AI-generated code
